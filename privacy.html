Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  |  Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The Chou Collection ("we," "us," or "our"), a sole proprietorship owned by Chung-Ih John Chou, collects, uses, and protects personal information, including information collected in connection with our SMS notification program.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, mobile phone number, email address
  • Property Information: Rental property address associated with your tenancy
  • SMS Consent Records: Date, time, and method of your opt-in consent to receive SMS messages
  • Communication Records: Records of SMS messages sent and received

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Send SMS notifications related to your tenancy, including rent reminders, maintenance updates, lease renewal notices, and property announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Maintain records of our communications as required by law
  • Notify interested parties of available rental properties
SMS Program: By opting in to our SMS program, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from The Chou Collection at the mobile number you provided. Message frequency varies based on property management needs. Message and data rates may apply.

3. SMS Opt-In and Opt-Out

Your consent to receive SMS messages is voluntary and collected through our web opt-in form. To stop receiving SMS messages at any time:

  • Reply STOP to any SMS message you receive from us
  • Contact us directly at choujohnc@gmail.com or 415-688-2818

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. To opt back in, reply START or re-submit the opt-in form on our website.

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at 415-688-2818.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • SMS Service Provider: We use Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Your phone number is shared with Twilio solely for message delivery purposes, subject to Twilio's privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.
  • Business Operations: With service providers who assist in our property management operations, under confidentiality obligations.

We do not share your mobile phone number or SMS consent with any third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. SMS consent records are stored securely and retained for the duration required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. SMS consent records are retained for a minimum of four years.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to legal requirements
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

8. California Privacy Rights

As a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to delete your personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights. To exercise these rights, contact us at choujohnc@gmail.com.
